üéØ What is Structured Output?

Structured Output means the AI returns responses in a consistent, predictable JSON format instead of free-form text.

This is crucial for LaughRx because your frontend needs to reliably separate and display the roast, diagnosis, and advice components!

Problems:

  • Hard to separate roast from advice
  • No severity indication
  • Inconsistent format
  • Difficult to parse programmatically

  • Clear component separation

  • Consistent every time
